Welcome to Protea Glen Youth Development Center

Word from the Director

Protea Glen Youth Development Center was duly registered on th 12th of July 2023 as a Non-Profit Company (NPC). the increase in demand, prompted the owners to look for people to assist. The further increaes in business and encouragement by colleagues, prompted the owners to register a legal form of business, hence the birth of Protea Glen Youth Development Center.

Our core business is to help school children with their homework, projects, assignments and tutoring at Protea Glen community. Our medium to long term goal is to become a leading provider capable of creating employment in Soweto and Gauteng as a whole.
